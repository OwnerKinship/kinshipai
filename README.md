# Kinship AI

Human-centered AI designed for connection, guidance, and support.

Kinship AI is an early concept exploring how artificial intelligence can help people navigate loneliness, stress, and life’s complex moments with compassion and practical guidance.

## Our Vision

Technology should strengthen human connection, not replace it.  
Kinship AI aims to create tools that feel supportive, respectful, and grounded in real emotional understanding.
